SUSTAINABILITY GOALS

  • 1.

    We believe in collaborative work and efforts. IP assets can facilitate partnerships and collaborations with governments, NGOs, and other stakeholders to promote sustainable agriculture and achieve SDGs collectively.
    Possibility and scope for Licensing agreements, joint ventures, and technology transfer initiatives can amplify impact of our innovations and accelerate progress towards multiple SDGs, particularly SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals).

  • 2.

    By providing accurate soil testing and crop recommendations and its accessibility through integrated technology, our innovations contribute to increasing agricultural productivity and promoting sustainable farming practices, by ensuring food security, improving nutrition, and promoting sustainable agriculture ( SDG 1 and 2)

  • 3.

    Through our Make in India developed soil testing devices, optimized, balanced nutrient and crop management is realized thereby reducing environmental impacts such as soil degradation, water pollution, greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating climate change.(SDG 13 and 15)

  • 4.

    Patents protect our innovative technologies related to soil testing devices, fertilizer recommendations, and crop recommendations. These patents encourage knowledge sharing and technology transfer, contributing to SDG 9 by fostering innovation and building sustainable infrastructure.

  • 5.

    Trademarks and designs help distinguish our soil testing devices and recommendations in the market, signalling quality and reliability to consumers. By promoting the adoption of sustainable agricultural practices through our branded solutions, contribute to SDG 8 and 12, which aims to ensure sustainable consumption, economic growth and production patterns.
